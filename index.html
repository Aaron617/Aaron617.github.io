I am a 2nd-year Ph.D. student at The University of Hong Kong (HKU-MMLAB) guided by Prof. Ping Luo (main) and Prof. Wenping Wang.

I am particularly focused on harnessing the potential of Large Language Models (LLMs) to enhance NLP tasks and advance Robotics applications. My current research agenda encompasses: 1. Development and optimization of LLM-based agents (AgentGen, HiAgent); 2. LLM-based planning (Tree-Planner, EmbodiedGPT); 3. Exploration of world modeling capabilities of LLMs (AgentGen).

I am open to research discussions and collaborations, feel free to get in touch at [mkhu at connect dot hku dot hk]!
