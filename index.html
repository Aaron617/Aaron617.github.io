I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student at The University of Hong Kong guided by Prof. Ping Luo (main) and Prof. Wenping Wang.

My research interest lies at the intersection of Embodied AI and Natural Language Processing (Large Language Models). Here are some interesting problem I am thinking about:   1) How to build lifelong evolving agent (LLM agent and RL agent);   2) Are Large Language Models good enough to model the world;   3) How to solve real-world problems with agent;

I am open to research discussions and collaborations, feel free to get in touch at [mkhu at connect dot hku dot hk]!
